Danger Zone

On some sites actions that are "dangerous" are put into a special zone for dangerous actions, or in other words, into a 'zone of danger'. clicking this link will play a short audio whenever you click on anything inside the danger zone. drag and drop it to your browser toolbar to create a bookmarlet for easy access on other sites.
Know issue: This will not work on github at the moment due to CSP limitetions that github has set.
